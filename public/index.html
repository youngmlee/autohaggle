AutoHaggle

Car buying for those of us who hate the hassle of haggle

Welcome to AutoHaggle, an automated haggling e-mail service designed to make the dealerships compete against each other while you sit at home!
This service aims to solve two problems: Removing the need to haggle in person and preventing your inbox from getting spammed from dealerships/car salesmen.

To start using AutoHaggle, simply fill out the form below with your information and we will take care of the rest!
Automatic bidding reports will be generated and sent to your e-mail 24/48/72 hours (3 total reports) from the time of form submission.
Please keep in mind, this is a car buying tool not a research tool. Make sure you know the exact car you wish to purchase (Tip: Build your car on the offical website first)
The more details you can provide, the better in terms of helping the dealerships give an accurate price quote.
